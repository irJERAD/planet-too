## The Collective Conscious  

Carl Jung describes the collective subconscious as an ocean beneath the waves.  

Each wave stands as an achitype:  
1. Ruler  
2. Creator  
3. Sage  
4. Innocent  
5. Explorer  
6. Rebel  
7. Hero  
8. Wizard  
9. Jester  
10. Everyman  
11. Lover  
12. Caregiver
